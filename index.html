About Me

Personal Information

Hola, I'm Miguel! I am a M.Sc. student in Data Science at the Sapienza University of Rome and a Data Scientist at Vivanta . I am very passionate about Social Networks and Data-Driven Modeling of Complex Phenomena. I am currently interested on several areas of research such as Opinion Dynamics, Information Diffusion, Inequality on Networks, Mobility Patterns, Graph Neural Networks and other interesting stuff."

In the past few years I completed my B.Sc. in Physics at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) and completed an extra-curricular research internship at the Complex Systems Institute at Ghent University in Belgium studying the dynamics of opinion formation in Social Networks.

In my spare time I enjoy watching almost any sport and playing Squash. When I was younger I used to represent my country in international tournaments and I was part of the Mexican National Team.
